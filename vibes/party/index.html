Night Timeline

  • 9-11pm: Dinner (seriously, eat late)
  • 11pm-1am: Pre-drinks at bars
  • 1-2am: Clubs open, still empty
  • 2-4am: Prime time, dance floor fills
  • 4-6am: Peak hours, locals arrive
  • 6am+: After-parties or beach sunrise
