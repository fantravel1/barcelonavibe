The Local Day

  • 8am: Coffee at the corner bar, standing at the counter, quick chat with the barista
  • 10am: Second coffee, maybe read the paper, still at the same bar
  • 12pm: Market shopping‚Äîdaily fresh ingredients, not weekly bulk buying
  • 1pm: Vermut hour begins. Aperitif and snacks at a neighborhood spot
  • 2:30pm: Lunch. The main meal. Takes at least an hour
  • 4pm: Siesta culture. Shops close. Streets quiet
  • 6pm: Back to life. Afternoon coffee or merienda (snack)
  • 9:30pm: Dinner. Never earlier. Usually with friends or family
