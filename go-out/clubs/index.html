Club Tips

Timing

  • Clubs open: ~midnight
  • Clubs fill up: 2-3 AM
  • Peak time: 3-5 AM
  • Closing: 5-6 AM
  • After-hours exist (ask around)

Money Savers

  • Guest lists: free/reduced before 1:30 AM
  • Thursdays: Erasmus nights, cheap entry
  • Pre-drink at bars, clubs are expensive
  • Some clubs free before midnight
  • Follow clubs on IG for promos
