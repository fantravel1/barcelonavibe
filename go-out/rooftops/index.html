Rooftop Tips

  • Best time: Arrive 1 hour before sunset for best spots
  • Reservations: Often required for sunset on weekends
  • Dress code: Smart casual minimum at hotel rooftops
  • Prices: Expect €12-18 for cocktails at hotel bars
  • Pro tip: Some hotel pools are open to non-guests for a day fee
