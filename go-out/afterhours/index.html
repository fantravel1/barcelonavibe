Survival Tips

  • Pace yourself: It's a marathon. Hydrate. Eat. Rest when you can.
  • Cash: Late venues often cash only
  • Transport: Metro opens at 5am weekdays, 24h weekends
  • Sunglasses: Bring them. You'll need them leaving at noon.
  • Be safe: Go with friends, watch your drinks, trust your instincts
