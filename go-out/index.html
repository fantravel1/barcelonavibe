Nightlife Essentials

Timing

  • Dinner: 9-10:30 PM (Spanish time)
  • Bars: 10 PM - 2:30 AM
  • Clubs open: Midnight
  • Clubs peak: 2-4 AM
  • Clubs close: 5-6 AM

Tips

  • Guest lists often mean free entry before 1 AM
  • Dress code at beach clubs (no flip flops)
  • Pre-drink at bars, clubs are expensive
  • Thursdays are big university nights
  • Download club apps for guest lists
