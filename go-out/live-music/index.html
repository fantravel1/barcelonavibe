Major Festivals

Primavera Sound

Early June. The world's best music festival. Indie, electronic, hip-hop - massive international lineups at Parc del Fòrum.

June

Sónar

Mid-June. Electronic music and digital arts. Daytime experimental showcases, nighttime massive raves.

June

Cruïlla

July. Rock, pop, and world music at Parc del Fòrum. More local feel than Primavera, family-friendly daytime.

July

Grec Festival

July. Performing arts festival across the city. Theater, dance, music in stunning outdoor venues.
