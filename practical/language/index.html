Language Guide

Barcelona is bilingual: Catalan and Spanish (Castellano). Everyone speaks Spanish, but starting with Catalan earns instant local respect.

Catalan Spanish Phrases Pronunciation
Basics

Essential Catalan

Catalan is NOT a Spanish dialect—it's closer to French and Italian. Using even a few words shows respect and opens doors.

Greetings

  • Hola - Hello
  • Bon dia - Good morning
  • Bona tarda - Good afternoon
  • Bona nit - Good night
  • Adéu - Goodbye
  • Fins després - See you later

Polite Phrases

  • Gràcies - Thank you
  • Si us plau - Please
  • Perdoni - Excuse me
  • Ho sento - Sorry
  • De res - You're welcome
  • Molt bé - Very good
Useful

Restaurant & Bar

Ordering

  • Una cervesa - A beer
  • Un vi - A wine
  • Un cafè - A coffee
  • L'aigua - Water
  • El compte - The bill
  • Quant costa? - How much?

Dining

  • Una taula per a dos - Table for two
  • La carta - The menu
  • Què recomaneu? - What do you recommend?
  • Bon profit! - Enjoy your meal!
  • Estava molt bo - It was very good
  • Salut! - Cheers!

Spanish Backup

Everyone speaks Spanish, so these work everywhere too:

  • Buenos días - Good morning
  • Por favor - Please
  • Gracias - Thank you
  • La cuenta - The bill
  • No entiendo - I don't understand
  • ¿Habla inglés? - Do you speak English?
  • ¿Dónde está...? - Where is...?
  • Vale - OK (very common)
