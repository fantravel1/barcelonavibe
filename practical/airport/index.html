Terminal Info

Terminal 1 (T1)

Main terminal. Most international flights, major airlines. Vueling, Iberia, British Airways, Lufthansa, etc.

Larger, more services, free shuttle to T2.

Terminal 2 (T2)

Smaller terminal split into T2A, T2B, T2C. Budget carriers: Ryanair, easyJet, Wizz Air mostly here.

Connected by free shuttle bus (15 min to T1).
