Spanish & Catalan

Barcelona speaks both. A few words in either language will earn you smiles and better service.

SpanishCatalanUseful

Basic Greetings

Spanish

  • Hola – Hello
  • Buenos días – Good morning
  • Buenas tardes – Good afternoon
  • Gracias – Thank you
  • Por favor – Please
  • Perdona – Excuse me
  • Lo siento – Sorry

Catalan

  • Hola – Hello (same!)
  • Bon dia – Good morning
  • Bona tarda – Good afternoon
  • Gràcies – Thank you
  • Si us plau – Please
  • Perdoni – Excuse me
  • Ho sento – Sorry

Restaurant Vocabulary

Ordering

  • La cuenta – The bill
  • Una cerveza – A beer
  • Agua sin gas – Still water
  • El menú del día – Set lunch

Useful Phrases

  • ¿Qué recomiendas? – What do you recommend?
  • Está muy bueno – It's very good
  • Sin gluten – Gluten-free
  • Soy vegetariano/a – I'm vegetarian

Getting Attention

  • Perdona – Excuse me
  • Cuando puedas – When you can
  • Oye/Oiga – Hey (informal/formal)
  • Make eye contact + small wave

Local Expressions

  • ¡Mola! – Cool! / Awesome!
  • Tío/Tía – Dude/bro (literally uncle/aunt)
  • ¿Qué tal? – How's it going?
  • Guay – Cool
  • Flipar – To be amazed (¡Estoy flipando!)
  • Currar – To work (slang)

Language Tips

  • Either works: Locals appreciate attempts in either Spanish or Catalan
  • English is common: Most service industry speaks English, especially with younger staff
  • Signs are bilingual: Street signs, metro, official things in both languages
  • Start in Spanish: If unsure, Spanish is understood by everyone
