SIM Cards & Data

Stay connected in Barcelona. Physical SIMs, eSIMs, and what to know about data in Spain.

Options

Best Choices

Physical SIM Cards

  • Orange: Best coverage, €10-20 for 10-50GB
  • Vodafone: Good tourist options
  • Lycamobile: Budget option, OK coverage
  • Where to buy: Airport, any phone shop, some tobacconists
  • Need: Passport or ID for registration

eSIMs (Recommended)

  • Airalo: Popular, easy setup, €5-15
  • Holafly: Unlimited data options
  • Nomad: Good Europe packages
  • Advantage: Install before arrival
  • Check: Phone must support eSIM

Good to Know

  • EU Roaming: If you have an EU phone plan, it works in Spain at no extra cost
  • UK Visitors: Post-Brexit, check your roaming charges—can be expensive
  • WiFi: Widely available—cafes, hotels, public spaces
  • Airport: Orange and Vodafone shops at Barcelona Airport arrivals
  • Activation: Usually instant, sometimes takes a few hours
  • Top-up: At supermarkets, phone shops, or online
