Getting Around

Metro, bus, taxi, bikes - how to navigate Barcelona efficiently. The city is very walkable, but knowing the transport system helps.

Essential

The Metro

Fast, frequent, covers everywhere you need.

Tickets & Passes

  • Single ticket: €2.55 - avoid unless desperate
  • T-Casual: €11.35 for 10 trips - best value for tourists
  • Hola BCN: Unlimited travel 2-5 days (€17.50-40) - worth it if you ride a lot
  • T-Casual works on metro, bus, tram, FGC trains within Zone 1
  • Buy at metro stations (machines accept cards)

Key Lines for Tourists

  • L3 (Green): Passeig de Gràcia, Diagonal, Sants - most useful line
  • L4 (Yellow): Barceloneta, Passeig de Gràcia, Joanic (Gràcia)
  • L1 (Red): Arc de Triomf, Plaça Catalunya, Hospital de Bellvitge
  • L5 (Blue): Sagrada Família, Diagonal, Sants
  • L9/L10: Airport connection (separate ticket €5.15)

Hours & Tips

  • Mon-Thu: 5am-12am
  • Fri-Sat: 5am-2am (Sat continues to Sunday morning)
  • Sunday: 5am-12am
  • Trains every 2-5 minutes in rush hour
  • Watch for pickpockets, especially L3 and La Rambla stations
Airport

From El Prat

Aerobus (Recommended)

  • Price: €7.75 single, €13.65 return
  • Time: 35 minutes to Plaça Catalunya
  • Frequency: Every 5-10 minutes
  • Hours: 5:35am-1:05am
  • Buy on bus or online (same price)
  • Best balance of cost and convenience

Other Options

  • Taxi: €39 flat rate (25-40 min)
  • Metro L9: €5.15, 50 min (T2 only)
  • RENFE train: €4.60, 25 min (T2 only)
  • Night bus N17: €2.55 (late night)
  • T1 and T2 connected by free shuttle
Options

Other Transport

Taxis

  • Yellow/black cars, metered
  • €2.30 base + €1.21/km
  • Use Free Now or Cabify apps
  • Uber exists but limited
  • Green light = available
  • Tip not expected

Bikes & Scooters

  • Bicing: City bikes (residents only)
  • Donkey Republic: Tourist bike rental
  • Lime/Tier: Electric scooters
  • Good bike lanes throughout
  • Beach promenade = great cycling
  • Helmets not required but smart

Buses

  • Same tickets as metro
  • Good for Montjuïc, Park Güell
  • Night buses (N-prefix) run 11pm-6am
  • TMB app shows real-time arrivals
  • Enter front, exit rear doors

Walking

  • City center is very walkable
  • Gothic to Barceloneta: 20 min
  • Rambla to Gràcia: 30 min
  • Most attractions clustered together
  • Comfortable shoes essential
  • Best way to discover the city
