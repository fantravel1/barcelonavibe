Best Time to Visit

Barcelona is great year-round, but each season has its character. Here's when to come for what you want.

Quick Answer

Best Months

  • May & June: Perfect weather, before peak crowds. Best overall.
  • September & October: Still warm, locals back, La Mercè festival. Excellent.
  • April: Pleasant, Sant Jordi festival (April 23). Shoulder season pricing.
  • Avoid if possible: August (hot, everything closed) and peak July/August (overcrowded beaches).
Seasons

Month by Month

Spring (Mar-May)

  • Weather: 15-22°C, occasional rain
  • Crowds: Moderate, increasing
  • Events: Sant Jordi (Apr), Primavera Sound (late May/Jun)
  • Beach: Too cold for most until May
  • Prices: Shoulder season, good deals

Summer (Jun-Aug)

  • Weather: 25-32°C, hot and humid
  • Crowds: Peak season, very busy
  • Events: Sónar (Jun), Pride (Jun), Cruïlla (Jul)
  • Beach: Perfect, but crowded
  • Note: August = locals leave, some closures

Fall (Sep-Nov)

  • Weather: 18-25°C, warm into October
  • Crowds: Moderate, manageable
  • Events: La Mercè (Sep), Jazz Festival (Oct-Dec)
  • Beach: Still swimmable in September
  • Why it's great: Locals back, city energy, good prices

Winter (Dec-Feb)

  • Weather: 10-15°C, mild, occasional rain
  • Crowds: Low (except holidays)
  • Events: Christmas markets, Three Kings (Jan 6)
  • Beach: Not for swimming
  • Why consider: Cheapest prices, no queues
