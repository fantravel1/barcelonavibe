Weather Guide

Mediterranean climate = mild winters, hot summers. 300+ sunny days per year. Here's the breakdown.

Overview

Best Times

Best Overall

April-June & September-October

Perfect weather, fewer crowds than summer. Beach-ready but not sweltering.

Best for Beach

June-September

Hot and sunny. Water warm enough to swim. Peak season = peak prices.

Best Budget

November-February

Cooler but still mild. Cheapest flights and hotels. No beach but great for culture.

What to Pack

Summer (Jun-Sep)

  • Light, breathable clothes
  • Swimsuit and cover-up
  • Sunscreen SPF 50+
  • Sunglasses and hat
  • Comfortable sandals
  • One light layer (AC is cold)

Winter (Nov-Mar)

  • Light jacket or coat
  • Layers (sweater + t-shirt)
  • Scarf (can be windy)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Small umbrella
  • No heavy winter gear needed
