Essential Apps

What locals actually use. Skip the tourist apps - these are the ones that matter.

Must Have

Transport

TMB Barcelona

Official metro and bus app. Real-time arrivals, route planning, service alerts. Essential.

Must Have

Free Now

The taxi app that works. Book cabs, see prices upfront, pay in-app. Better than Uber here.

Taxis

Cabify

Uber alternative that actually works in Barcelona. Fixed prices, good cars, reliable.

Rideshare

Google Maps

Still the best for walking directions and transit. Download offline maps before you arrive.

Navigation
Food

Eating & Delivery

TheFork (ElTenedor)

Restaurant reservations with discounts up to 50%. The local OpenTable. Most restaurants are on it.

Reservations

Glovo

Food delivery, groceries, pharmacy - anything delivered. The dominant delivery app in Barcelona.

Delivery

Just Eat

Alternative to Glovo. Sometimes has different restaurants. Good for comparing prices.

Delivery

Too Good To Go

Rescue food from restaurants and bakeries at 70% off. Great for budget travel and reducing waste.

Budget
Nightlife

Going Out

Resident Advisor

Club events, DJ lineups, ticket purchases. Essential for electronic music scene.

Clubs

Xceed

Guest lists for Barcelona clubs. Free or reduced entry. Works for most mainstream venues.

Guest Lists

Dice

Concerts, gigs, live music events. Curated selection, fair resale prices if you can't go.

Concerts

Fever

Events, experiences, pop-ups. From immersive theater to rooftop cinema. Discover unique things.

Experiences
Useful

Other Essentials

Bizum

Instant payments between people. How Spaniards split bills. Needs Spanish bank account though.

Payments

Revolut/Wise

Best exchange rates, no foreign transaction fees. Essential for non-Euro travelers.

Money

WhatsApp

Everyone uses WhatsApp here. Restaurant reservations, hostel communication, meeting people.

Communication

Meetup

Language exchanges, hiking groups, professional networking. Good for meeting locals and expats.
