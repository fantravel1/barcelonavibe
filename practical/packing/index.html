What to Pack

Barcelona's Mediterranean climate and stylish locals mean packing smart. Here's what you actually need.

Comfortable Walking Shoes

Cobblestones will destroy fancy shoes. Bring broken-in sneakers or supportive sandals. You'll walk 10-15km daily minimum.

Sunglasses

Mediterranean sun is intense year-round. Good sunglasses are essential, not optional. Bring polarized if you can.

Crossbody Bag

Pickpockets are real. Crossbody or front-carry bag only. No backpacks on crowded metro. Anti-theft features are smart.

By Season

Summer (Jun-Sep)

  • Light breathable fabrics
  • Swimsuit (obviously)
  • Reef-safe sunscreen SPF 50
  • Hat for midday sun
  • Light cardigan for AC blasting

Winter (Dec-Feb)

  • Medium jacket (not heavy coat)
  • Layers‚Äîmornings cold, afternoons mild
  • Light scarf
  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Small umbrella (occasional rain)

Spring (Mar-May)

  • Layers are key
  • Light jacket for evenings
  • Mix of short/long sleeves
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sunscreen (sun stronger than expected)

Fall (Oct-Nov)

  • Similar to spring
  • Rain jacket (October especially)
  • Warmer layers for November
  • Still warm enough for terraces
  • Beach possible in October

Leave at Home

  • Heels: Cobblestones will ruin them and you
  • Heavy winter coat: Even winter rarely needs it
  • Lots of cash: Card accepted everywhere, cash invites pickpockets
  • Flashy jewelry: Draws attention, makes you a target
  • Formal wear: Barcelona is casual, even nice restaurants
