Booking Tips

Reservations

  • Disfrutar/Tickets: 2+ months ahead
  • Other starred: 2-4 weeks ahead
  • Lunch is often easier to book
  • Check for cancellations close to date
  • Book directly on restaurant websites

What to Expect

  • Tasting menus: 2-3 hours minimum
  • Wine pairing adds €50-100+
  • Smart casual minimum (no shorts/flip flops)
  • Credit card guarantee usually required
  • Dietary restrictions: tell them when booking
