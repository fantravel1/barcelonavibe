Late Night Tips

Best Areas

  • Raval: Most options, sketchiest vibes
  • Gothic: Tourist kebabs, late bars
  • Gràcia: Better quality, closes earlier
  • Port Olímpic: After beach clubs

Pro Tips

  • McDonald's on La Rambla is 24h
  • Most supermarkets close by 10pm
  • Pakistani corner shops sell snacks late
  • Night buses (Nitbus) run until 6am
