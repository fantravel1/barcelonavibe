Food Tips

Meal Timing

  • Breakfast: 8-10 AM
  • Lunch: 1:30-3:30 PM
  • Vermouth: 12-2 PM (weekends)
  • Dinner: 9-11 PM
  • Late night: 2-4 AM

Red Flags

  • Photos on the menu (usually bad)
  • Touts standing outside
  • "Tourist menu" signs
  • Beachfront restaurants with touts
  • Anywhere on La Rambla
