Know

Paella Rules

  • It's a lunch dish: Real Spanish people eat paella at lunch, never dinner
  • Minimum 2 people: Always ordered to share
  • 20-30 minute wait: Made fresh. If it comes fast, it was pre-made
  • Look for socarrat: The crispy bottom layer is the prize
  • Try fideu√†: The Catalan version with noodles‚Äîoften better
  • Avoid La Rambla: 90% of paella there is tourist garbage

What to Order

Rice Dishes

  • Paella de mariscos: Seafood paella
  • Arroz negro: Black rice with squid ink
  • Fideu√†: Catalan noodle paella
  • Arroz a banda: Rice cooked in fish broth
  • Paella mixta: Meat and seafood (not traditional)

How to Eat

  • Eat directly from the pan
  • Use your spoon, work from outside in
  • Fight for the socarrat (crispy bottom)
  • Pair with white wine or cava
  • Aioli on the side is acceptable
