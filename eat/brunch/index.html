Brunch Tips

Timing

  • Peak: 12-2pm weekends
  • Best time: 11am to avoid waits
  • Reservations: Always for popular spots
  • Many places do brunch daily, not just weekends

Budget

  • Budget: €12-18 per person
  • Mid-range: €18-25 per person
  • Bottomless: €25-40 per person
  • Coffee often charged separately (€2-4)
