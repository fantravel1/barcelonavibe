Coffee Guide

Spanish Coffee Terms

  • Café solo: Espresso
  • Cortado: Espresso + splash of milk
  • Café con leche: Espresso + hot milk
  • Café americano: Espresso + water
  • Flat white: Say "flat white" - they know

Pricing

  • Local bar espresso: €1-1.50
  • Specialty espresso: €2-3
  • Specialty flat white: €3-4
  • Pour-over: €4-5
  • No tipping expected
