Tapas Etiquette

  • Order rounds: 2-3 dishes at a time, then more. Let the meal unfold.
  • Standing bars: Often better food. Eat at the bar, leave when done.
  • Share everything: That's the whole point. Fork in, no plates.
  • Timing: Lunch tapas 1-3pm, dinner tapas 8-11pm. Respect the rhythm.
