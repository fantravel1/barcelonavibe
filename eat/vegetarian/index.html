Veggie Tips

Ordering Tips

  • "Soy vegetariano/a" = I'm vegetarian
  • "Sin carne ni pescado" = No meat or fish
  • Ask about stock (caldo) - often meat-based
  • Jamón is in everything - specify no ham
  • Tortilla española is vegetarian (eggs)

Traditional Veggie Options

  • Pa amb tomàquet: Bread with tomato
  • Patatas bravas: Usually vegan
  • Escalivada: Roasted vegetables
  • Pimientos de padrón: Fried peppers
  • Gazpacho: Cold tomato soup
