Cruïlla Festival

Barcelona's crossroads of music. World sounds, rock, indie, and local favorites on multiple stages. Family-friendly by day, party by night.

Early July Parc del Fòrum World Music Family-Friendly
Overview

What Makes It Special

Cruïlla (meaning "crossroads" in Catalan) lives up to its name. Where else do you get African rhythms, British rock legends, local Catalan bands, and Latin beats on the same lineup? The festival celebrates musical diversity without the pretension.

  • Dates: Usually first weekend of July (3 days)
  • Venue: Parc del Fòrum (same as Primavera Sound)
  • Capacity: ~60,000 daily
  • Vibe: More relaxed than Primavera, very local crowd
  • Special: Cruïlla XXS stage for kids and families
Tickets

Getting In

Ticket Prices

  • 3-Day Pass: ~€120-160
  • Day Ticket: ~€55-70
  • VIP: ~€200+
  • Kids under 10: Free
  • Prices lower than Primavera/Sónar

How to Buy

  • Official site: cruillabarcelona.com
  • On sale: Usually March/April
  • Rarely sells out completely
  • Day tickets often available
  • Family passes available

Festival Tips

Prepare

  • Sun protection essential (July heat)
  • Comfortable shoes
  • Cash + card accepted
  • Food options are good
  • Metro runs late during festival

With Kids

  • Cruïlla XXS has kid activities
  • Ear protection for little ones
  • Afternoon heat can be intense
  • Strollers allowed but not ideal
  • Family-friendly until ~8pm
