Sónar Festival

The electronic music festival. 3 days of cutting-edge sounds, tech showcases, and massive night raves. Since 1994.

Mid-June Electronic Day + Night Technology Art
Format

Day vs Night

Sónar by Day

  • Where: Fira Montjuïc (city center)
  • Hours: 12pm-12am
  • Vibe: Experimental, tech talks, art
  • Price: ~€65-85/day
  • Showcases, installations, workshops
  • Easier to navigate, good food options

Sónar by Night

  • Where: Fira Gran Via (L'Hospitalet)
  • Hours: 9pm-7am
  • Vibe: Full-on rave, massive stages
  • Price: ~€85-110/night
  • Big-name headliners
  • Shuttle buses from city center
Tickets

Ticket Options

  • Full Festival (Day+Night): ~€235-295
  • Day Only (3 days): ~€140-180
  • Night Only (3 nights): ~€190-250
  • Single Day/Night: Available closer to event
  • Sónar+D (conference): Additional ~€200

Buy at sonar.es. Early bird in January. Prices increase over time. Usually sells out.

Festival Tips

Preparation

  • Book hotel near Fira Montjuïc
  • Comfortable shoes essential
  • Earplugs for front of stage
  • Download the Sónar app
  • Pace yourself - it's a marathon

Night Strategy

  • Shuttles from Plaça Espanya
  • Or taxi/rideshare (€15-20)
  • Sleep during the day
  • Eat before going
  • Know your headliner times

Off-Sónar Parties

The unofficial parties during Sónar week are legendary. Every club hosts special events. Some are better than the festival itself.

  • Check: Resident Advisor, RA Guide Barcelona
  • Venues: Razzmatazz, Nitsa, Input, Moog
  • Beach parties: Various daytime events
  • Secret parties: Follow local DJs on social media
  • Book early - everything sells out
