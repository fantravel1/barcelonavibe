One Week Barcelona

Seven days to fall in love. Enough time to go deep. Day trips, hidden neighborhoods, local rhythms.

7 DaysCompleteDay Trips

Week Overview

Day 1: Arrival

Check in, Gothic Quarter orientation, first tapas, early night to beat jet lag.

Day 2: Gaudí

Sagrada Familia (morning), Casa Batlló, Passeig de Gràcia shopping, Gràcia dinner.

Day 3: Culture

Picasso Museum, El Born neighborhood, beach afternoon, Barceloneta seafood.

Day 4: Day Trip

Montserrat monastery OR Costa Brava beaches. Return for late dinner.

Day 5: Park Güell

Morning at Park Güell, Bunkers sunset, serious night out. Sleep in tomorrow.

Day 6: Local Life

Late brunch, Poblenou/Sant Antoni exploration, vermouth hour, cooking class option.

Day 7: Departure

Market visit, last-minute shopping, favorite neighborhood revisit. Leave with promises to return.

Week Tips

  • Build in rest: Don't schedule every moment. Leave room for wandering.
  • One day trip max: Barcelona itself deserves most of your time.
  • Neighborhoods matter: Each has personality. Spend real time in 2-3 favorites.
  • Last day flexibility: Save one experience for final morning. End on high note.
