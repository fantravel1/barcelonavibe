3️⃣

Beach & Views

Morning: Park Güell (book 9:30). Afternoon: Beach, Barceloneta. Evening: Bunkers sunset, farewell dinner.

Relaxed

Make It Work

  • Book immediately: Sagrada Familia and Park Güell sell out weeks ahead.
  • Stay central: Born, Gothic, or Eixample. Walking distance to everything.
  • Skip: La Rambla (walk through, don't linger), tourist restaurants.
  • Prioritize: If cutting, skip Park Güell before Sagrada Familia. Never skip Sagrada.
