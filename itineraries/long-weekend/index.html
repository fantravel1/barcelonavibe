Long Weekend Barcelona

Four perfect days. All the highlights, some hidden gems. Arrive Thursday, leave Sunday transformed.

Day by Day

Thursday: Arrival & Gothic

Settle in, walk Gothic Quarter and Born. Get lost intentionally. Tapas dinner at El Xampanyet. Drinks at rooftop bar.

Architecture
3️⃣

Saturday: Beach & Culture

Morning beach time. Lunch at Barceloneta. Afternoon Picasso Museum. Sunset at Bunkers del Carmel. Club night!

Full Day
4️⃣

Sunday: Slow Morning

Brunch somewhere excellent. La Boqueria market stroll. One more Gaudí (Casa Batlló?). Depart with memories.

Relaxed

Pro Tips

  • Book ahead: Sagrada Familia, Park Güell, popular restaurants. 2+ weeks minimum.
  • Stay central: Gothic, Born, Eixample. Walk everywhere. Maximize time.
  • Embrace late dining: Dinner at 9pm feels early. Adjust your rhythm.
  • One splurge: Pick one amazing meal. Don't try to do everything fancy.
