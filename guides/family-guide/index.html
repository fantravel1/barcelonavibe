Practical Tips

Spanish Kid Culture

Spain is incredibly child-friendly. Kids out late is normal. Restaurants welcome families. Children run around plazas while parents dine. Don't stress about noise.

Dining Tips

Eat early (8pm) when restaurants are empty. Tapas style means kids can try different things. Most places have high chairs. Patatas bravas are usually kid-approved.
