Cocktail Tips

Getting In

  • Paradiso: Book online or queue
  • Bobby's Free: Ring bell, know password
  • Two Schmucks: Walk in early
  • Hotel bars: Open to all
  • Weekdays are always easier

Price Guide

  • Standard bars: €10-14
  • Top bars: €14-18
  • Hotel bars: €16-22
  • Paradiso/Sips: €18-25
  • No tipping expected
