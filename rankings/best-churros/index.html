Churros 101

  • Churros vs Porras: Churros are thin, porras are thick. Both good.
  • The chocolate: Should be thick enough to coat. Not watery.
  • Best times: Breakfast, merienda (4pm snack), or 5am post-club
  • Don't dip: Some people sugar them instead of chocolate. Valid.
