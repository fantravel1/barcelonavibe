Best Brunch 2026

The definitive ranking. From healthy bowls to bottomless mimosas - Barcelona's top 12 brunch spots.

1

Flax & Kale

Healthy • El Raval/Eixample • €€

The pioneer of healthy brunch in Barcelona. Flexitarian menu, stunning presentation, great coffee. Multiple locations. Book weekends.

2

Federal Café

Aussie-Style • Gothic/Sant Antoni • €€

Australian owners who brought proper brunch to Barcelona. Excellent eggs, avocado toast done right, specialty coffee. The OG.

3

Brunch & Cake

Instagram-Worthy • Eixample • €€

Most photogenic brunch in Barcelona. Pink everything, flower walls, elaborate presentations. The food is actually good too.

4

Satan's Coffee Corner

Coffee-First • El Raval • €€

Best coffee in Barcelona, simple but excellent food. Hipster without trying too hard. Come for coffee, stay for eggs.

5

Milk Bar & Bistro

Boozy Brunch • Gothic Quarter • €€

Classic American-style brunch with cocktails. Eggs Benedict done right. The go-to for hungover weekends in the Gothic.

6

Nomad Coffee

Specialty Coffee • Poblenou/Gòtic • €€

Own-roasted specialty coffee, simple quality food. Serious about coffee, unpretentious about everything else.

7

Rooftop Smokehouse

American BBQ Brunch • El Born • €€€

Weekend brunch with BBQ twist. Smoked meats, American portions, rooftop terrace. Hearty hangover cure.

8

Chök

Chocolate & Brunch • El Born • €€

Chocolate makers who do brunch. Amazing pastries, creative dishes, killer hot chocolate. For those with a sweet tooth.

9

Picnic

All-Day • El Born • €€

All-day brunch, garden terrace, good coffee. Kid-friendly, dog-friendly. Relaxed El Born vibe.

10

The Benedict

Eggs Specialist • Gràcia • €€

Eggs Benedict in every variation imaginable. Does one thing and does it well. Perfect poached eggs guaranteed.

11

Alsur Café

European-Style • El Born • €€

European-meets-American brunch. Good coffee, nice terrace on Plaça Sant Pere. Popular with locals.

12

Can Dendê

Brazilian • Poble Sec • €€

Brazilian brunch - something different. Açaí bowls, tapiocas, tropical vibes. A refreshing change from the norm.

Brunch Tips

When to Go

  • Weekdays: Walk in anytime
  • Weekends: 10-11am or after 2pm
  • Peak chaos: 12-2pm Saturdays
  • Book ahead for groups
  • Most serve until 4pm

Price Guide

  • Coffee + pastry: €5-8
  • Full brunch plate: €12-18
  • Brunch + drinks: €20-30
  • Bottomless options: €30-45
  • Most places don't do tips
