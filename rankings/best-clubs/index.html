Best Clubs 2026

The definitive ranking. From underground techno to mainstream bangers - Barcelona's top 15 clubs.

1

Razzmatazz

5 Rooms • Poblenou • €15-20

The institution. Five rooms, five vibes - from indie to techno to pop. Something for everyone. Where Barcelona goes out.

2

Nitsa Club

Techno/House • Apolo • €15-18

Barcelona's most respected electronic music night. Inside Sala Apolo. Underground, credible, legendary. Saturday nights only.

3

Moog

Techno • El Raval • €12-15

Tiny, sweaty, legendary. Two rooms - proper techno downstairs, indie upstairs. The authentic Barcelona underground experience.

4

Pacha Barcelona

House/Commercial • Port Olímpic • €20-30

The Ibiza brand's Barcelona outpost. Glossy, beachfront, bottle-service available. Commercial house with big-name DJs.

5

Input

Techno/Electronic • Poblenou • €10-15

Industrial space, serious sound system. Where Barcelona's techno heads go. Part of the Razzmatazz complex but separate entrance.

6

Sala Apolo

Varied • Paral·lel • €12-20

Historic ballroom, now concert venue and club. Different nights have different vibes. Check the schedule - it varies wildly.

7

Macarena Club

Techno/House • Gothic Quarter • €10-12

Tiny box, huge reputation. On Plaça Reial. Great bookings for its size. Sweaty, intimate, open late. A Barcelona institution.

8

Opium

Commercial • Port Olímpic • €20-30

Beach club that goes late. International crowd, commercial music. Good for groups who want easy fun by the sea.

9

Sidecar

Rock/Indie • Plaça Reial • €8-12

Live music venue and club. Rock, indie, alternative. Basement vibe under Plaça Reial. For people who don't do techno.

10

La Terrrazza

Open Air • Poble Espanyol • €15-20

Summer only. Open-air clubbing in Poble Espanyol. House and techno under the stars. One of Barcelona's best summer experiences.

11

City Hall

House/Techno • Eixample • €12-18

Former theater, excellent sound. Quality house and techno bookings. More grown-up crowd. Near Plaça Catalunya.

12

Eclipse at W Hotel

Upscale Lounge • Barceloneta • €20+

26th floor views, dress code, bottle service. More lounge than club but open late. For special occasions or impressing someone.

Clubbing Tips

When to Go

  • Clubs open: 12am-1am
  • Peak time: 2am-4am
  • Closing: 5am-6am
  • Best nights: Thursday-Saturday
  • Arrive before 1am for free/cheap entry

Getting In

  • Guest lists via club social media
  • Resident Advisor for events
  • Dress code varies - check ahead
  • Groups of guys may struggle
  • Mixed groups enter easier
