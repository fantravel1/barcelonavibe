Best Rooftops 2026

The definitive ranking. From sunset cocktails to late-night vibes - Barcelona's top 12 rooftop bars.

1

La Isabela (Hotel 1898)

Pool & Views • Las Ramblas • €€€

Rooftop pool, 360° views of Gothic Quarter and port. Colonial-style terrace. Best overall experience. Book for sunset.

2

Eclipse (W Hotel)

26th Floor • Barceloneta • €€€€

Highest rooftop in Barcelona. Mediterranean views from the iconic sail building. Dress code enforced. Worth it for special occasions.

3

Terraza Martinez

Panoramic • Montjuïc • €€€

Unreal city views from Montjuïc hillside. More restaurant than bar, but the terrace is spectacular. Paella with a view.

4

Hotel Pulitzer Terrace

Central • Plaça Catalunya • €€

Most central rooftop. Intimate atmosphere, good cocktails. Popular with locals and hotel guests. Arrive early for seats.

5

Sky Bar (Grand Hotel Central)

Infinity Pool • Via Laietana • €€€

Stunning infinity pool overlooking the city. Non-guests can access the bar area. Cathedral views. Sophisticated crowd.

6

The Roof (Hotel Barcelona Edition)

Design • Eixample • €€€

Ian Schrager's hotel rooftop. Sleek design, excellent cocktails, Sagrada Família views. The cool crowd's choice.

7

Azimuth (Hotel Arts)

Beachfront • Port Olímpic • €€€€

At the iconic Frank Gehry fish. Mediterranean views, sophisticated atmosphere. Premium prices, premium experience.

8

La Dolce Vitae (Majestic Hotel)

Classic Luxury • Passeig de Gràcia • €€€€

Old-school glamour on the most famous street. Pedrera and Casa Batlló views. Where Barcelona's elite drinks.

9

Hotel Neri Rooftop

Intimate • Gothic Quarter • €€€

Small, hidden, romantic. Overlooks Gothic Quarter rooftops. Not the biggest views but the most atmospheric. Perfect for dates.

10

Casa Bonay Rooftop

Hip • Gran Via • €€

Where the creative crowd goes. Good music, reasonable prices, great atmosphere. More bar than hotel terrace feel.

11

La Terraza del Claris

Elegant • Eixample • €€€

Museum-hotel's rooftop. Egyptian artifacts meet cocktails. Pool for guests, bar for everyone. Unique vibe.

12

Generator Rooftop

Budget • Gràcia • €

Best budget rooftop. Hostel bar open to all. Young crowd, cheap drinks, decent views. Proof you don't need €15 cocktails.

  • After dinner: 10pm-12am
  • Weekdays: Less crowded
  • Summer: Book reservations
  • Winter: Some close or move indoors
