Paella Rules

  • Lunch only. Real paella is a lunch dish. Dinner paella = tourist trap.
  • Minimum 2 people. Paella is meant to share. Order per person.
  • 20+ minute wait. Good paella is made to order. Fast paella = pre-made.
  • Skip La Rambla. Every paella there is a tourist trap. Every single one.
  • Try fideu√†. Same concept but with short noodles. Often better than paella.
  • Socarrat matters. The crispy bottom is the best part. Ask for extra.
