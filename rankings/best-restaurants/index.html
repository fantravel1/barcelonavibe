Best Restaurants 2026

The definitive ranking. From Michelin stars to hidden tapas bars - Barcelona's top 15 places to eat.

1

Disfrutar

3 Michelin Stars • Eixample • €200-300

Former El Bulli chefs. #2 in World's 50 Best. Mind-bending molecular gastronomy that's still delicious, not just clever.

2

Tickets

Experiential • Paral·lel • €90-150

The Adrià brothers' tapas theater. Impossible to book, worth every effort. Fun, surprising, delicious.

3

Quimet y Quimet

Tapas • Poble Sec • €20-35

Standing room only. Four generations. The montaditos are legendary. Proof that simple done perfectly is everything.

4

Can Paixano

Cava Bar • Barceloneta • €15-25

Chaotic, standing, loud, perfect. €1.50 cava, incredible seafood. The Barcelona experience distilled.

5

Cinc Sentits

2 Michelin Stars • Eixample • €150-220

Modern Catalan tasting menus. More accessible than Disfrutar. Exceptional wine pairing.

6

Cal Pep

Seafood Tapas • El Born • €50-80

Counter seating, watch Pep's team work. Best seafood tapas in the city. No reservations, arrive early.

7

Bar Brutal / Can Cisa

Natural Wine Bar • El Born/Sant Antoni • €40-60

Best natural wine bar in Barcelona. The food matches the wine. Knowledgeable, unpretentious.

8

7 Portes

Rice/Seafood • El Born • €40-70

Since 1836. Barcelona's most famous rice restaurant. Historic setting, serious cooking. Book the paella ahead.

9

La Pepita

Creative Tapas • Gràcia • €30-50

Vintage decor, creative tapas, late kitchen. The bikini truffle is famous. Reserve or queue.

10

Cervecería Catalana

Tapas • Eixample • €30-50

Always packed for good reason. Excellent quality, fair prices. The shellfish is outstanding.
