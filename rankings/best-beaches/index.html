Best Beaches 2026

All 9 Barcelona beaches ranked. From touristy to local, party to peaceful - find your spot.

1

Platja de Bogatell

Local Favorite • Poblenou • 600m

The locals' choice. Wide, clean, less crowded than Barceloneta. Good chiringuitos, volleyball courts. The best balance of everything.

2

Platja de la Mar Bella

Alternative • Poblenou • 500m

LGBT+ friendly, nudist section, younger crowd. Best chiringuito (Chiringuito Mar Bella). The alternative beach that's still central.

3

Platja de la Nova Icària

Family/Chill • Port Olímpic • 400m

Calmer water (protected by breakwater), good facilities, family-friendly. Near Port Olímpic restaurants. The easy choice.

4

Platja de Barceloneta

Iconic/Touristy • Barceloneta • 1100m

The famous one. Most crowded but also most iconic. Great people-watching, walkable from city center. Experience it once, then go elsewhere.

5

Platja del Llevant

Peaceful • Forum • 375m

Newest beach, furthest from center. Quietest option. Near Forum area which has interesting architecture. Worth the trek for peace.

6

Platja de la Nova Mar Bella

Quiet • Poblenou • 420m

Between Mar Bella and Llevant. Fewer facilities but fewer people. Local feel, good for a quieter day.

7

Platja de Sant Miquel

Central • Barceloneta • 420m

Southern end of Barceloneta. Near Club Natació swimming facilities. Slightly less crowded than main Barceloneta stretch.

8

Platja de Sant Sebastià

Historic • Barceloneta • 660m

Oldest beach, near W Hotel. More locals, older crowd. Good swimming club nearby. Less scene than main Barceloneta.

9

Platja del Somorrostro

Party • Port Olímpic • 500m

Right at Port Olímpic. Near the clubs, busy at night. Not for relaxing - for transitioning from beach to nightlife.

Beach Tips

Best Times

  • Early morning: Empty, calm water
  • 10am-12pm: Filling up
  • 2pm-5pm: Peak crowds
  • 6pm-sunset: Golden hour, perfect
  • Weekdays: Always less crowded

Know Before

  • Free showers at all beaches
  • No glass containers allowed
  • Watch your belongings
  • Lifeguards: 10:30am-7pm (summer)
  • Water quality: generally excellent
