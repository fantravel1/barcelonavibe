Figueres

The Dalí Theatre-Museum. The world's largest surrealist object, created by the master himself.

55 min AVEDalíSurrealism

The Museum

Designed by Dalí as a total artwork, not just a museum. His largest collection, his crypt, his masterpiece. Eggs on the roof, melting clocks inside. Mind-bending at every turn.

  • Entry: ~€14-18, book online to skip queues
  • Time needed: 2-3 hours minimum
  • Night visits: Summer only, magical experience

Getting There

By Train

  • AVE: 55 min from Barcelona Sants, ~€15-25
  • Regional: ~2 hours, cheaper
  • Walk: 15 min from Figueres-Vilafant station

Combine With

  • Cadaqués: Dalí's home, stunning coast
  • Girona: Stop on the way back
  • Púbol: Dalí's castle for wife Gala
