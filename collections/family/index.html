Family Beaches

Best for Families

  • Nova Ic√†ria: Calm water, good facilities
  • Bogatell: Less crowded, wide sand
  • Castelldefels: 20 min train, shallower water
  • Free showers at all beaches
  • Lifeguards 10:30am-7pm (summer)

Beach Tips

  • Go early (before 11am) for best spots
  • Rent umbrellas at chiringuitos
  • Watch belongings - don't leave unattended
  • Bring water shoes for rocky areas
  • Pack snacks - beach food is pricey

Family Tips

Dining with Kids

  • Kids eat late in Spain - it's normal
  • Most restaurants welcome children
  • Tapas = easy kid ordering
  • Highchairs widely available
  • Kids menus not always available

Getting Around

  • Metro: Free under 4, strollers OK
  • Many stairs - plan elevator exits
  • Walkable if staying central
  • Taxis have child seats (request)
  • Rental strollers available
