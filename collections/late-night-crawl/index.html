Survival Guide

  • Pace yourself. The night is long. Spanish drinking culture is marathon, not sprint.
  • Nitbus runs all night. Routes N0-N17 cover the whole city. Google Maps knows.
  • Stay together. Phone battery, meeting points, all that basic stuff.
  • Watch your stuff. Pickpockets work late-night crowds. Front pockets only.
  • Bring cash. Some late-night spots are cash only.
  • Hydrate. Water between drinks. Your future self will thank you.
