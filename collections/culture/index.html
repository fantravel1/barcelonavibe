Culture Tips

Book Ahead

  • Sagrada Família: 2-4 weeks
  • Park Güell: 2-7 days
  • Casa Batlló: 2-5 days
  • Picasso Museum: 1-3 days
  • Book earliest morning slots for fewer crowds

Free Days

  • Sundays 3pm: Many museums
  • First Sunday: Several free all day
  • Picasso: Thursday 4-7pm
  • MACBA: Saturday 4-8pm
  • Check museum websites for current schedule
