Budget Barcelona

You don't need money to love Barcelona. Free museums, cheap eats, and priceless experiences.

FreeCheap EatsTips

Free Things

Free Museums

  • Sundays 3-8pm: Picasso, MNAC, MUHBA
  • First Sunday: Many museums all day
  • MACBA plaza: Free skate watching
  • Fundació Tàpies: Free terrace access

Free Experiences

  • Beach (obviously)
  • Park Güell free zone
  • Bunkers del Carmel sunset
  • Gothic Quarter wandering
  • Gaudí facades (exterior free)

Cheap Eats

  • Menú del día: €12-15 for 3 courses + drink at lunch
  • Bocadillos: €3-5 sandwiches at any bar
  • Markets: Eat at market bars, half the price of restaurants
  • Supermarkets: Mercadona, Bon Preu for picnic supplies
  • Vermouth hour: Cheap drinks + free snacks at bodegas
  • Pintxos: Carrer Blai—€1-2 per pintxo, eat standing

Money Tips

  • T-Casual card: €11.35 for 10 metro rides
  • Skip La Rambla restaurants: Tourist prices everywhere
  • Drink at home first: Pre-drinks save €€€ on clubbing
  • Guestlists: Free club entry before 1:30am
  • Tap water: Ask for "agua del grifo"—it's safe and free
  • Visit in winter: Hotels 40-50% cheaper than summer
