Beach to Club

The perfect Barcelona day: Mediterranean morning, golden afternoon, electric night. Sun, sand, sunset, and dance floor.

Full Day Beach Party Summer Vibes
Morning

Beach Time

  • 10:00 — Late breakfast at home or quick coffee
  • 11:00 — Arrive at Bogatell or Nova Icària (less crowded than Barceloneta)
  • 11:00-14:00 — Beach time: swim, read, nap, volleyball
  • 14:00 — Lunch at a chiringuito (beach bar). Order sangria.

Pro tip: Bring a beach tent or umbrella. The midday sun is intense.

Afternoon

Recharge Mode

  • 15:30 — Head back to accommodation
  • 16:00 — Shower, rest, maybe a siesta (essential!)
  • 18:00 — Wake up, coffee, get ready
  • 19:00 — Head out for sunset drinks

Critical: The siesta is non-negotiable if you're going out. Trust the process.

Evening

Sunset & Dinner

  • 19:30 — Sunset drinks: W Hotel terrace, Barceloneta rooftops, or Bunkers
  • 21:00 — Dinner in El Born or Barceloneta (book ahead on weekends)
  • 23:00 — Post-dinner drinks: cocktail bar or local spot
  • 00:30 — Head towards the club area
Night

Club Time

  • 01:00 — Arrive at the club (not before)
  • 01:00-05:00 — Dance, drink, meet people
  • 05:00 — Grab food (kebab, pizza, or hit up a 24h bakery)
  • 06:00 — Watch sunrise on the beach (optional but magical)

Best clubs near beach: Pacha, Shôko, Opium (touristy but fun), or take metro to Razzmatazz.

Packing List

Day Bag

  • Swimsuit, towel, sunscreen
  • Book or phone entertainment
  • Water bottle
  • Cash for chiringuito
  • Change of clothes for evening

Night Out

  • ID (passport works best)
  • Card + some cash
  • Phone charger/power bank
  • Comfortable but stylish shoes
  • Light jacket (AC in clubs is cold)
