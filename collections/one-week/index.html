One Week

Seven days is enough to really know Barcelona. Day trips, deep dives, and time to just wander.

7 DaysCompleteDay Trips

Day 1: Arrival & Gothic

Settle in, explore Gothic Quarter, La Rambla (just to see it), dinner in El Born. Early night to recover.

Day 2: Gaudí Day

Sagrada Família (morning), Passeig de Gràcia facades, Park Güell (afternoon), Bunkers sunset.

Day 3: Beach & Nightlife

Barceloneta beach, seafood lunch, siesta, rooftop drinks, tapas crawl, clubbing (Razzmatazz/Apolo).

Day 4: Montserrat Day Trip

Early train to Montserrat. Monastery, hiking, views. Return for late dinner in Gràcia.

Day 5: Culture Deep Dive

Picasso Museum, El Born exploring, Fundació Miró, MNAC at sunset. Wine bars evening.

Day 6: Neighborhoods

Gràcia morning (markets, plazas), Sant Antoni lunch, Poblenou afternoon (street art), El Raval night.

Day 7: Sitges or Costa Brava

Beach day trip: Sitges (40min, easy) or Costa Brava (longer, more dramatic). Return for farewell dinner somewhere special.
