Survival Tips

Essentials

  • Sunscreen (reapply constantly)
  • Water bottle (refill stations exist)
  • Hat and sunglasses
  • Light cotton clothes
  • Fan (locals carry them)

Pro Tips

  • Siesta 2-5pm (everything closes anyway)
  • Dinner after 9:30pm when it cools
  • Night swims are magical
  • August = locals leave, half the city closed
  • Book beaches outside the city for space
