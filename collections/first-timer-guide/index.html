First Timer

Your first Barcelona trip? Here's everything you need to know to do it right.

BeginnerTipsMust-Know

Must Do

  • Sagrada Família: Book 2-4 weeks ahead. Non-negotiable.
  • Beach day: This is a Mediterranean city. Use it.
  • Tapas crawl: Multiple bars, one dish each. The real way.
  • Gothic Quarter: Get lost in the medieval maze.
  • Sunset at Bunkers: Best view, totally free.
  • Late dinner: Embrace the 9pm+ schedule.

Common Mistakes

  • Eating on La Rambla: Tourist traps. Every single one.
  • Arriving at clubs before 1am: Empty and embarrassing.
  • Not booking Sagrada Família: Will sell out.
  • Overpacking schedule: Barcelona rewards wandering.
  • Ignoring siesta: Nap if you're going out at night.
  • Speaking only English: "Hola" and "gracias" go far.

Know Before

Timing

  • Lunch: 2-4pm
  • Dinner: 9-11pm
  • Clubs: 1-6am
  • Shops close: 2-5pm (siesta)

Safety

  • Pickpockets: Real, be aware
  • Violence: Very rare
  • Scams: La Rambla area
  • Overall: Very safe city
