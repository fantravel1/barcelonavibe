Date Ideas

  • Get lost in the labyrinth at Parc del Laberint
  • Sunset sailing from Port Olímpic
  • Flamenco show at Tablao de Carmen
  • Wine tasting in El Born
  • Picnic in Ciutadella Park
  • Cooking class together

Honeymoon Highlights

  • Stay at Hotel Neri or Mercer
  • Dinner at Disfrutar or Tickets
  • Day trip to Costa Brava
  • Spa day at Aire de Barcelona
  • Sunrise at the beach (just the two of you)
  • Rooftop hopping at sunset
