Photo Tips

Best Light

  • Sunrise: Empty landmarks (7-8am)
  • Golden hour: 1-2 hours before sunset
  • Blue hour: Just after sunset
  • Midday: Harsh shadows - avoid
  • Overcast = soft, even light

Pro Tips

  • Monday mornings = fewest tourists
  • Clean your lens (sand, fingerprints)
  • Portrait mode for people shots
  • Lower angles = more dramatic
  • Don't forget to just enjoy the view
