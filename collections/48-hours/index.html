48 Hours

Two days, maximum Barcelona. Every hour optimized for the best experience.

2 DaysMust-SeesOptimized
Day 1

Gaudí & Gothic

Morning

  • 9:00 — Sagrada Família (book 9am slot, arrive 8:45)
  • 11:30 — Walk to Hospital Sant Pau (Modernista gem, often overlooked)
  • 12:30 — Metro to Passeig de Gràcia, see Casa Batlló & La Pedrera facades

Afternoon

  • 14:00 — Lunch in Gràcia (Cal Pep, Roig Robí, or local spot)
  • 16:00 — Explore Gràcia's plazas and boutiques
  • 18:00 — Park Güell (book afternoon slot for sunset light)

Evening

  • 20:00 — Sunset drinks at Bunkers del Carmel
  • 21:30 — Dinner in El Born
  • 23:30 — Cocktails or early night (save energy for Day 2)
Day 2

Beach & Nightlife

Morning

  • 10:00 — Brunch at Federal Café or Flax & Kale
  • 11:30 — Gothic Quarter exploration: Cathedral, Plaça Reial
  • 13:00 — La Boqueria market (go to the back stalls)

Afternoon

  • 14:00 — Beach time at Barceloneta or Nova Icària
  • 17:00 — Return to hotel, siesta/rest
  • 19:00 — Sunset at W Hotel terrace or beach chiringuito

Night

  • 21:00 — Tapas crawl in El Born or Sant Antoni
  • 23:30 — Cocktails at Paradiso or Dry Martini
  • 01:30 — Club: Razzmatazz, Apolo, or Nitsa
