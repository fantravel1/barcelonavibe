72 Hours

Three days is the sweet spot. Enough time to see the highlights and discover your own favorites.

3 DaysLong WeekendBalanced

Day 1: Gaudí & Modernisme

  • Morning: Sagrada Família (book 9am slot)
  • Midday: Walk Passeig de Gràcia, see facades
  • Afternoon: Park Güell, explore Gràcia neighborhood
  • Evening: Bunkers sunset, dinner in Gràcia

Day 2: Old City & Beach

  • Morning: Gothic Quarter, Cathedral, Picasso Museum
  • Midday: El Born exploration, lunch
  • Afternoon: Beach time at Barceloneta
  • Evening: Tapas crawl, cocktails, clubbing

Day 3: Montjuïc & Neighborhoods

  • Morning: Fundació Miró, MNAC
  • Midday: Montjuïc gardens, cable car
  • Afternoon: Sant Antoni or Poble Sec exploring
  • Evening: Special dinner, farewell drinks
