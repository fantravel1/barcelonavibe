Català

Barcelona's native tongue. Different from Spanish, deeply tied to Catalan identity. Understanding it enriches your visit.

CultureIdentityLanguage

Basic Phrases

Greetings

Hola (Hello), Bon dia (Good morning), Bona tarda (Good afternoon), Bona nit (Good night)

Essential

Politeness

Gràcies (Thank you), Si us plau (Please), Perdó (Sorry/Excuse me), De res (You're welcome)

Polite

Useful

Sí (Yes), No (No), Adéu (Goodbye), Quant costa? (How much?), Parla anglès? (Do you speak English?)

Helpful

Language Tips

  • Everyone bilingual: Catalans speak both Catalan and Spanish. English widely spoken too.
  • Street signs: Often in Catalan. "Carrer" = street, "Plaça" = square.
  • Appreciation: Trying Catalan shows respect. Even "gràcies" is appreciated.
  • Identity: Catalan language is tied to regional identity. It's political and personal.
