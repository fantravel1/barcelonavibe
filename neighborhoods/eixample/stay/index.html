Why Eixample

  • Best for: Architecture lovers, LGBTQ+ travelers, dining enthusiasts
  • Transport: Multiple metro lines, walkable to most attractions
  • Vibe: Elegant, quieter than old town at night, residential feel
  • Left vs Right: Esquerra (left) more local, Dreta (right) more tourist/shopping
  • Price range: Mid to high, but good value compared to Gothic/Born
