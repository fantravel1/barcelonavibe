Village Bars

Can Paixano

Cava bar legend. Standing room, cheap cava, amazing sandwiches. Not beach but essential Barceloneta.

Jai-Ca

Old fisherman's bar. Tiny, loud, authentic. Vermouth and seafood. Cash only. Real Barceloneta.

Absenta

Absinthe bar near beach. Modernist decor, strong drinks. Quiet alternative to beach chaos. Evening vibes.

Santa Marta

Wine bar on main drag. Local wines, good tapas. Escape the tourist traps. Reasonable prices.
