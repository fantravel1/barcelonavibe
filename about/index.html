About BarcelonaVibe

We're building the guide to Barcelona that we wished existed.

Our Mission

BarcelonaVibe is the guide we wished existed when we first came to Barcelona. Not the Gaudí-only, tourist-trap-filled guides that send you to overpriced paella on La Rambla. The real guide.

We're built for young locals, Erasmus students, backpackers, festival-goers, and travelers who want Barcelona alive, not packaged. We cover where to actually go out, where young people actually eat, and the beach-to-club transitions that make Barcelona special.

Our Principles

  • No tourist traps. If locals don't go there, neither do we.
  • Evidence-based. Every recommendation is verified and regularly updated.
  • Respect the city. We're aware of over-tourism and write responsibly.
  • Safety first. We never recommend unsafe areas or practices.
  • No pay-to-play. Venues can't pay for recommendations.

Evidence Tiers

Every listing on BarcelonaVibe includes an evidence tier:

  • Tier A: Firsthand local verification
  • Tier B: Reputable local media sources
  • Tier C: Verified traveler reports
  • Tier D: Never published (unverified)
