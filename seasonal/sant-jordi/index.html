Sant Jordi

Catalonia's day of love. Books for her, roses for him (or however you prefer). The most beautiful day in Barcelona.

April 23RomanticBooks

The Tradition

Saint George slayed the dragon, a rose grew from its blood. Men traditionally give women roses; women give men books. Today, everyone exchanges both. It's Valentine's Day meets World Book Day, uniquely Catalan.

La Rambla transforms into a sea of book and flower stalls. Authors sign books. Couples stroll. It's magical.
