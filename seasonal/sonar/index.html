Since 1994—the festival that put Barcelona on the electronic music map. Advanced music and multimedia art.

Day vs Night

Sónar by Day

Fira Montjuïc venue. Noon to midnight. More experimental, art installations, tech demos, conferences. Shaded venues, more chill.

Best for: Music discovery, art, daytime vibes

Sónar by Night

Fira Gran Via venue (L'Hospitalet). Midnight to 7am. Big headliners, massive stages, peak energy. The main event for most.

Best for: Dancing, big names, all-night party

Sónar Tips

  • Combo tickets: Day + Night passes are best value if you want both
  • Sleep strategy: Day (noon-midnight) → sleep → Night (midnight-7am)
  • Off-Sónar: The unofficial parties around the city are legendary
  • Transport: Shuttle buses between venues included with ticket
  • Explore: Smaller stages often have the best discoveries
  • Hydrate: Free water points—use them, especially at Night
