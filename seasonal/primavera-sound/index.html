Primavera Sound

Barcelona's legendary music festival. World-class lineup, Mediterranean setting, unforgettable energy.

JuneFestivalMusic

The Festival

What to Expect

Late May/early June. Parc del Fòrum venue by the sea. Eclectic lineup from indie to electronic to hip-hop. Known for curated quality over mainstream names. Runs until 4-5am. Party continues in the city after.

Key Info

  • Duration: Main days Thu-Sat + city events
  • Tickets: Sell out fast, buy early
  • Prices: €200-300+ for full festival
  • Getting there: Metro L4 El Maresme-Fòrum

Survival Tips

  • Accommodation: Book months ahead, prices triple during festival
  • Pacing: Marathon not sprint—festival goes until dawn
  • Sunscreen: Afternoon sun is brutal, no shade at main stages
  • Comfortable shoes: The venue is massive, you'll walk kilometers
  • Cash: Card works but queues faster with cash for drinks
  • Phone battery: Bring portable charger, you'll need it
  • Rest: Don't party city night before—you'll regret it
