What's a Chiringuito?

A chiringuito is a beach bar. They range from simple wooden shacks serving drinks to full-service restaurants with DJs. Most are seasonal (April-October) and temporary structures.

Tip: Chiringuitos are great for drinks and atmosphere. For food, quality varies wildly - some are excellent, many are overpriced tourist traps. Choose carefully.

Chiringuito Tips

Pricing

  • Beer: €4-6
  • Cocktails: €10-15
  • Food: €15-25 mains
  • Day bed: €30-100+ with minimum spend
  • Credit cards usually accepted

Pro Tips

  • Book day beds in advance (summer weekends)
  • Sunset = most crowded time
  • Weekdays are much calmer
  • Avoid eating at the club-style ones
  • Walk further from Barceloneta for better value
