Where

Rental Spots

Base Nàutica Mar Bella

Municipal watersports center on Mar Bella beach. SUP, kayaks, windsurfing, sailing courses. Best prices, legit operation. Open May-October.

Location: Platja de la Mar Bella

Port Olímpic Marina

Multiple operators for sailing, jet skis, boat rentals. More expensive, more tourist-oriented. Good for group charters and sailing trips.

Location: Port Olímpic

Barceloneta Beach

Various rental stands in summer. Quality and prices vary. Good for spontaneous SUP or kayak rental. Negotiate prices.

Location: Along Barceloneta promenade

Sailing Schools

For proper sailing lessons, try Centre Municipal de Vela or private schools like Vela Barcelona. Multi-day courses available.

Location: Port Olímpic, Port Vell

Water Sports Tips

Best Times

  • Morning: Calmest water, best for SUP
  • Afternoon: Wind picks up (good for sailing)
  • Sunset: Beautiful but book ahead
  • Season: May-October for most activities

What to Know

  • Mediterranean is calm - good for beginners
  • Water temp: 20-26°C in summer
  • Book sailing trips in advance
  • Sunscreen is essential
  • Bring water and snacks for longer activities
