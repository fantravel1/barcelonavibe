Barcelona Beaches

4.5 km of Mediterranean coastline. 9 distinct beaches. From packed Barceloneta to quiet Llevant - find your perfect stretch of sand.

9 Beaches 4.5 km Free May-Oct Mediterranean

The Basics

Barcelona's beaches run from Barceloneta in the southwest to Llevant in the northeast. All beaches are free, public, and have lifeguards in summer. The water is clean (Blue Flag certified) and the Mediterranean means calm waves.

Season: May-October. Best months: June and September (warm but not packed). Peak: July-August is crowded everywhere.

Beach Tips

What to Know

  • Lifeguards: 10:30am-7pm (summer)
  • Free showers on all beaches
  • No glass containers allowed
  • Vendors sell drinks (semi-legal)
  • Watch belongings - theft happens

Best For

  • Families: Nova Icària, Bogatell
  • Locals: Bogatell, Mar Bella
  • Party: Barceloneta, Somorrostro
  • Quiet: Llevant, Nova Mar Bella
  • Nudist/LGBTQ+: Mar Bella
