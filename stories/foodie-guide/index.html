Rhythm

Meal Timing

Barcelona runs on a different clock. Fighting it will frustrate you. Embrace it.

  • Breakfast (8-10am): Coffee and croissant, quick and light
  • Mid-morning (11am): Second coffee, maybe a bikini (ham & cheese toast)
  • Vermut (12-2pm): Pre-lunch drinks and snacks, social ritual
  • Lunch (2-4pm): Main meal of the day, often 3 courses
  • Merienda (5-7pm): Afternoon snack, coffee break
  • Dinner (9-11pm): Lighter than lunch, never before 9pm
