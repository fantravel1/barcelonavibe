Barcelona Museums

World-class collections. From Picasso to contemporary art. Barcelona's cultural heart awaits.

Must Visit

Essential

Museu Picasso

Five medieval palaces. Formative works, Blue Period. Book ahead online. Free first Sunday. Born neighborhood.

Art

Fundació Joan Miró

Montjuïc hillside setting. Miró's life work. Building itself is art. Gardens included. City views bonus.

Modern

MACBA

Contemporary art in Raval. Richard Meier building. Skaters outside, art inside. Free Saturday afternoons.

Contemporary

More Museums

MNAC

National Art Museum of Catalonia. Romanesque masterpieces. Palau Nacional setting. City views from terrace.

CosmoCaixa

Science museum. Flooded Amazon forest inside. Great for kids. Outside center but worth trip.

MUHBA

City history museum. Roman ruins underground. Walk ancient streets. Plaça del Rei location.

Museu Marítim

Maritime museum in medieval shipyards. Royal galley replica. Free Sunday afternoons.
