City Beaches

Famous

Barceloneta

The iconic one. Crowded but convenient. Full facilities, chiringuitos, life. Metro accessible. Tourist central.

Iconic

Nova Icària

Best for families. Calm water, nearby playground. Less crowded than Barceloneta. Olympic port adjacent.

Families

Mar Bella

Nudist section, LGBTQ+ friendly. Younger, alternative crowd. Good chiringuitos. Further but worth it.

Alternative

Beyond City

Sitges Beaches

30 min south by train. Multiple beaches, charming town. LGBTQ+ friendly. Day trip essential. Summer packed.

Costa Brava

1-2 hours north. Crystal clear water, rocky coves. Tossa de Mar, Cadaqués. Car helps but buses exist.

Castelldefels

20 min by train. Wide beach, less crowded. Watersports available. Good for families. Local favorite.

Ocata (El Masnou)

30 min north by train. Blue flag beach. Much quieter. Good beach bars. Local secret.
