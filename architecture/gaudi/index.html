Antoni Gaudí

Barcelona's visionary architect. Nature-inspired masterpieces that define the city. UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

ModernismeUNESCOGenius

Major Works

#1 Must See

Sagrada Familia

Unfinished masterpiece under construction since 1882. Book 2+ weeks ahead. Towers extra. Morning light best. Life-changing.

€26-40

Park Güell

Garden city with mosaic dragon. Timed entry required for monumental zone. Go at sunrise. City views, whimsical beauty.

€10

Casa Batlló

Dragon-back rooftop, bone balconies. Most photogenic facade. Night visits available. AR experience innovative.

€35-45

More Gaudí

La Pedrera (Casa Milà)

Wavy facade, warrior chimneys. Rooftop sunset tours. Less crowded than Batlló. €25-33

Palau Güell

Early Gaudí. Raval location. Rooftop chimneys preview Park Güell. Less known, less crowded. €12

Casa Vicens

Gaudí's first house. Gràcia neighborhood. Recently opened to public. Colorful tiles, Moorish influence. €18

Casa Calvet

Restaurant inside Gaudí building. Dine in his design. Reserve ahead. Expensive but unique experience.
