Cocktail Tips

Pricing

  • Standard bars: €8-12
  • Craft cocktail: €12-15
  • World-class: €15-20
  • Gin tonic: €8-15
  • No tipping required

Pro Tips

  • Book Paradiso and Sips in advance
  • Gin tonics are served in copa glasses
  • Most bars open after 7pm
  • Dress smart casual for upscale spots
  • Happy hours are rare in Barcelona
