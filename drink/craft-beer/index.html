Beer FAQ

What's a "caña"?

A small draft beer (about 200ml). The standard Spanish beer order. Say "una caña, por favor" and you'll get the house lager. Cheap (€1.50-3) and refreshing.

Is craft beer popular in Barcelona?

Growing fast. Ten years ago, finding an IPA was hard. Now there are dozens of dedicated craft bars and several local breweries. Still a niche compared to wine/vermut culture, but thriving.

Can I do a brewery tour?

Estrella Damm offers factory tours (book online). Craft breweries like Garage and Edge have taprooms but don't always do formal tours. Check websites for events.
