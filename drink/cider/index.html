Sidra

Spanish cider poured from height, drunk in one go. Asturian and Basque traditions in Barcelona.

AsturianBasqueTraditional

The Tradition

Spanish sidra is poured from height ("escanciar") to aerate it. You drink quickly, leaving the dregs. It's social, fun, and slightly messy. Different from sweet commercial ciders.

Basque vs Asturian: Similar tradition, slightly different styles. Both are dry, tart, refreshing.
