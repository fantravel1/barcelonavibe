Booking Tips

Price Range

  • Mid-range boutique: €120-200/night
  • Design hotels: €200-350/night
  • Luxury boutique: €350-600/night
  • Book direct for perks
  • Shoulder season (April, Oct) = best rates

Best Areas

  • El Born: Trendy, walkable, foodie heaven
  • Eixample: Modernist buildings, central
  • Gothic: Historic, atmospheric, lively
  • Gràcia: Local feel, quieter nights
