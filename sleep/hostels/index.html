Hostel Tips

Pricing

  • Dorm (8-12 bed): €15-25/night
  • Dorm (4-6 bed): €25-40/night
  • Private room: €50-90/night
  • Summer weekends are most expensive
  • Book 1-2 weeks ahead in peak season

What to Know

  • Bring a padlock (or buy one there)
  • Earplugs are essential
  • Check-in often starts at 2-3pm
  • Luggage storage usually available
  • Female-only dorms exist at most hostels
