Booking Tips

  • Book early for summer - June-August fills up fast
  • Check tourist tax - €0.65-4.90/night added at checkout
  • Apartments need licenses - Only book legal rentals
  • Location matters - Central is worth paying for nightlife access
  • Read recent reviews - Quality changes fast in Barcelona
