Under €80 Private Room Pension Basic Central

Budget Stay Options

Don't want a hostel dorm but can't afford €150 hotels? Barcelona has pensions (old-school guesthouses), budget hotel chains, and no-frills options that offer private rooms for €40-80/night.

Trade-off: smaller rooms, basic amenities, maybe shared bathrooms. Worth it for privacy and quiet.

Types

Budget Categories

Pensions (€30-60)

Traditional Spanish guesthouses. Often family-run, in old buildings. Shared or private bathrooms. Very basic but characterful. Look for ones updated in last 10 years.

  • Pensión Alamar (Gothic, €40-55)
  • Pensión Ciudadela (El Born, €35-50)
  • Hostal Centric (Eixample, €45-65)

Budget Chains (€50-80)

Reliable, predictable, clean. No charm but no surprises. Good for a night or two when you just need a bed.

  • Ibis Budget (multiple locations)
  • Hotel Sidorme
  • Motel One (design-forward budget)
  • Travelodge Barcelona

Where to Stay Cheap

Best Budget Areas

  • Raval: Cheapest central, edgier
  • Eixample (far edges): Good value, safe
  • Sants: Near station, local prices
  • Poble Sec: Up-and-coming, cheaper

Avoid Paying More

  • Book 2+ weeks ahead
  • Avoid weekends if possible
  • May-June and Sept-Oct = sweet spot
  • Check Booking.com Genius discounts
  • Consider apartments for 4+ nights
